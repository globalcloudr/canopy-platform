Direction A — Loosen the grid, keep the kit. Warmer neutral (#fbf8f2) replaces indigo. Accent swaps from Tailwind blue to a deeper, more distinctive navy. Ink becomes warmer. Radii drop from 28px to 8–12px. The generic gradient hero is replaced by a first-name greeting. The "eyebrow → title → muted" triple-decker is gone — sections just have a title. Cards are flatter and smaller. Services list is a flush table, not a rounded container.
