Canopy is a connected school growth platform built to help schools organize, publish, communicate, and grow.

Canopy brings together the tools schools need to manage digital publishing, brand assets, marketing content, communications, outreach, and performance visibility in one connected ecosystem.
Built specifically for schools and education organizations, 

## Canopy helps schools:

protect and organize photos, brand assets, and structured content
publish school information, classes, and programs online
manage design and collateral production
distribute newsletters and social content
share publications and success stories
support communication through chat tools
monitor performance through connected reporting and insights


Canopy is designed to become the operating platform for school marketing, communications, publishing, and community engagement.
In simple terms: Canopy helps schools organize, publish, communicate, and promote everything they need to grow.

## Brand Architecture

Canopy is the umbrella brand and platform identity for the ecosystem.

The Canopy brand brings together a family of connected tools, modules, and publishing systems, each designed to serve a specific role in school marketing, communications, publishing, and operations.

This creates a clear and scalable product family where Canopy functions as the master platform brand and each product has a focused, practical role.

usecanopy.school functions as both the public-facing home of Canopy and the operating portal through which clients access the ecosystem.

## Domain Strategy

The recommended umbrella domain is:
usecanopy.school

This domain supports the long-term Canopy platform identity and signals a strong school-sector focus. It is broad enough to support multiple products and strong enough to become the public-facing home of the ecosystem.

Product-specific domains, apps, and WordPress plugins can continue to support individual products where needed, while usecanopy.school serves as the central portal and entry point to the platform.

## Platform Model

Canopy is organized as a connected school growth platform with a central portal and a family of products, modules, and plugins.

1. Asset Foundation

PhotoVault by Canopy

PhotoVault is the asset foundation of the Canopy platform.

It provides schools with a secure, organized environment for managing:

approved photos
photo libraries and albums
logos
brand assets
share-ready media
structured visual content


PhotoVault supports the rest of the Canopy ecosystem by serving as the trusted asset and brand layer for websites, newsletters, social media, collateral design, and communications.

Positioning:
The simple, powerful way to manage school marketing and brand assets.

2. Web Publishing

Canopy Website

Canopy Website is the web publishing and CMS product within the Canopy ecosystem.
It is designed to help schools build integrated websites and publish classes, programs, and public-facing information more effectively.

Canopy Website supports:
CMS platforms for schools
school website development
class and program publishing
public information management
integration with systems like ASAP through API
WordPress-based plugins and integrations
seamless public-facing digital experiences


A key component of Canopy Website is the existing WordPress plugin for ASAP integration, which makes it easier for schools to display classes and programs on their websites.
Canopy Website gives schools a stronger digital foundation for program visibility, enrollment support, and content management.

3. Design Publishing

Canopy Create

Canopy Create is the design and collateral production product within the Canopy ecosystem.

It helps schools create, manage, revise, and organize the visual materials they use to communicate with students and communities.

Canopy Create includes:

class catalogs
brochures
flyers
campaign materials
revisions
creative production workflows
design request and approval systems

This gives schools a structured design production environment connected to their broader communications and publishing system.

4. Publication Management

Canopy Publish

Canopy Publish is the publication distribution and management product within the Canopy ecosystem.

It is focused on managing and embedding Issuu-based publications within school websites and publishing environments.

Canopy Publish supports:

publishing Issuu publications inside website and CMS experiences
managing digital brochures, catalogs, and guides
embedding publications into school sites
supporting clients through a WordPress plugin for managing publications

This product helps schools present polished long-form publications in a more integrated and manageable way.

5. Community Communication

Canopy Community

Canopy Community is the email newsletter and school-community communication product within the Canopy ecosystem.

It is designed to help schools manage recurring email communication through a newsletter portal connected to the platform.

Canopy Community supports:

newsletter workflows
editorial and communication management
recurring school-to-community communication
email distribution workflows
a WordPress plugin for sending email newsletters

This product helps schools strengthen communication with students, families, staff, and the broader community.

6. Outreach and Storytelling

Canopy Reach

Canopy Reach is the social outreach and storytelling product within the Canopy ecosystem.

It combines social media scheduling with success story distribution and automation.

Canopy Reach supports:

social media scheduling tools
social campaign planning and coordination
success story automation
content distribution across channels
ongoing outreach and visibility workflows

This product helps schools amplify their stories, increase visibility, and extend the reach of their communications.

7. Communication and Knowledge

Canopy Assistant

Canopy Assistant is the communications and knowledge product within the Canopy ecosystem.

It supports conversational access to structured information and is designed to grow into a broader communication platform for schools.

Canopy Assistant supports:

structured-data querying
chatbot support
customer service use cases
internal knowledge access
internal and external communication workflows

This product turns organized school information into accessible, conversational support.

8. Measurement and Visibility

Canopy Insights

Canopy Insights is the analytics, reporting, and visibility product within the Canopy ecosystem.

It gives schools a clearer understanding of campaign activity, outreach performance, and communication impact.

Canopy Insights supports:

analytics dashboards
campaign monitoring
outreach reporting
newsletter and social visibility
connected measurement across school growth activities

This product helps schools understand what is working and where to focus next.
